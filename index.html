Game Features

Choose from a variety of weapons, from basic guns to powerful explosives, and upgrade them as you progress.

Game Controls

Learn how to navigate the game and use your skills effectively to survive the monster onslaught.

WASD

Movement

Use the WASD keys to move your character in all directions.

Mouse

Aim & Shoot

Use your mouse to aim and left-click to shoot your current weapon.

1-5

Weapon Selection

Press number keys 1-5 to quickly switch between your equipped weapons.

R

Reload

Press R to reload your current weapon when it's out of ammo.

Space

Jump

Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or gain tactical advantage.

E

Interact

Press E to interact with objects, pick up weapons, or open doors.
